Features

Secure

It helps to store data securely and immutably, providing security and trust in the authenticity of the data.

Traceability

It enables traceability of cordyceps, providing transparency and accountability for buyers looking for ethically sourced products.

Certification

A feature allowing for the certification and quality assurance of cordyceps products can help build trust and promote high-quality products.

Supply chain

Cordychain can be used to track cordyceps from collection to distribution, improving transparency and efficiency.
